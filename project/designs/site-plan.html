Home Page Design

Header

Logo: Prominently display the "Time Well Spent"

Navigation Menu: Home | Search | Book | About

Main

Hero Section: A captivating image or video showcasing the concept of traveling and exploring cities.

Tagline: A concise and memorable tagline, such as "Explore More, Travel Smarter."

Brief Description: A brief overview of the website's purpose and benefits to users.

Key Features: Highlight the main features of the website, such as:

  • Flight search with extended layover options
  • City recommendations based on layover duration
  • Personalized itineraries
  • User reviews and ratings

Call to Action: A prominent "Search Flights" button to encourage users to start their journey.

Footer

Social Media Links: Icons for connecting with the website on social media platforms.

Contact Information: Email address, phone number, and physical address.

Legal Information: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and other legal documents.
